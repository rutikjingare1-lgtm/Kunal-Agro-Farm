About Kunal Agro Farm

Kunal Agro Farm is a trusted agricultural logistics and vegetable supply company committed to delivering fresh, high-quality farm produce from farmers to markets with speed, safety, and reliability.

We specialize in transportation and distribution of fresh vegetables while maintaining their freshness throughout the supply chain.

Mission

To bridge the gap between farmers and customers through dependable logistics, timely delivery and transparent business practices.

Vision

To become one of Maharashtra's most trusted fresh vegetable transportation companies.

Founder & Managing Director

Driving excellence in agricultural transportation and fresh vegetable supply.

Mr. Kunal Kailas Sapkal

Founder & Managing Director

Mr. Kunal Kailas Sapkal founded Kunal Agro Farm with a vision of providing reliable, timely and efficient transportation solutions for fresh vegetables.

His dedication to quality, customer satisfaction and dependable logistics has helped connect farmers directly with wholesalers, retailers, supermarkets, hotels and restaurants.
